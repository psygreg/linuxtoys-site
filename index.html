              For you
            

            

              A multitool that just works, with a comfortable, practical and
              simple graphical interface that looks good and gets the job done.
              It also follows the theme of your system!
            

            Get it here!
          

          
        

      


      

        

          

            
            

              

                For professionals
              

              

                With the CLI mode, taking a long time to set up computers for
                your customers will be a thing of the past. Just list all
                features you need installed in the manifest file, and let
                LinuxToys do the job for you. You can save your custom manifest
                file to use it to standardize your customers' systems. And don't
                worry: it won't apply resources that are not meant for the
                target!
